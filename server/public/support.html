Need help? Here are answers to common questions and how to get in touch.

Adding recipes

You can add recipes by pasting text, pasting a recipe link, uploading a photo, or pasting a video URL. The app will parse ingredients and steps. For best results, use clear recipe text or a direct link to a recipe page.

Subscription (Cooked Pro)

Cooked Pro unlocks unlimited saved recipes and full AI parsing. Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. To restore a purchase, use Restore Purchases on the paywall screen. For billing or refunds, contact Apple Support or use the report a problem link in your App Store receipt.

Privacy & data

Your saved recipes are stored on your device. We use third-party services for recipe parsing and subscriptions. See our Privacy Policy for details.
